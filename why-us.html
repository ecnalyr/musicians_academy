12 Benefits of becoming an Academy student

12 Benefits of becoming an Academy student

Convenience

We are located at 5858 South Padre Island Drive inside Sunrise Mall #37C next to Planet Fitness. Just 15 minutes from anywhere in the South side and near all the dining and shopping centers.


One Stop Shop

This saves our students time and money as one family can take multiple instruments on the same day. Other studios may offer multiple instruments but we are the only one to offer all 8 of the most popular instruments.


Qualified

We have 12 music teachers all with either University training or professional performance experience. Our music teachers are dedicated to learning about your personal musical goals and helping you achieve them. In addition to their teaching credentials our teachers have warm personalities, are extremely friendly and dedicated to teaching you the style of music that you want to learn.


Availability

Monday through Friday 1:00 p.m. - 9:00 p.m. • Saturday 12:00 p.m. - 9:00 p.m. • Sunday 1:00 p.m. - 6:00 p.m. We are sure our flexible hours will allow us to schedule lesson times that work best for you!


Excellence

We've been teaching students with a standard of professional integrity since 2005. A family oriented environment with over 180 students currently enrolled. Our mission is to train a new generation of musicians young and old.


Comfortability

Instead of waiting in a music retail store or someone's home, our clients enjoy a comfortable lobby with TV, reading materials, and Cubicles for studying. We also provide Free WiFi, bottled water, and candy.


Watch Them Learn

We have an open door policy. A parent can choose to sit in the teaching studio, view the lesson on our lobby security monitor, or view the lesson in the Monitoring room with a large 2 way mirror.


Teaching Studios

Our studios are stocked with everything you need for lessons including amps, acoustic and electronic drum sets, pianos, computers, soundproofing, observation windows, and more. All you need to bring is your instrument. We have spent thousands of dollars on our academy to offer you the best learning environment.


Yearly Recital

Every December our students have the option of performing in our Winter recital. Students are not required to perform but many choose to.


No Hidden Fees

Musicians Academy does not require semester minimums for lessons. Every student enrolled is on a month to month basis.


Pre-screened staff

Recommended by Guitar Center and South Texas Music Mart. All of our instructors have passed national background checks for your safety and ours.


Safe and Secure

Each lesson is recorded with a State of the Art Monitoring system with 14 cameras on constant record mode.
