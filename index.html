The Benefits of Learning Music

Music Education improves Success in School, Higher IQ’s, & Develops Life long Skills. Researchers have found that children given Music lessons significantly improved in their spatial- temporal IQ scores compared to children who received computer lessons or no lessons. Abstract reasoning skills for learning Math and Science increased. Studying music encourages self-discipline, diligence, commitment, goal setting traits that carry over into intellectual pursuits, effective study, and work habits. Develops Self-Confidence, Self-Esteem, Coordination, Concentration, and Creativity. U.S. Department of Education data reports that consistent high levels of involvement in instrumental music studies over the middle and high school years show "significantly higher levels of mathematics proficiency by grade 12.”
