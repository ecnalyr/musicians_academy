The Benefits of Learning Music

Music Education improves Success in School, Higher IQ’s, & Develops Life long Skills. Researchers have found that children given Music lessons significantly improved in their spatial- temporal IQ scores compared to children who received computer lessons or no lessons. Studying music encourages self-discipline, diligence, commitment, goal setting traits that carry over into intellectual pursuits, effective study, and work habits.
